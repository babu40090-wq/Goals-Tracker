1Mon
Monday — Energise & ResetNon-veg allowed · Anti-inflammatory focus
☀️ Breakfast · 7:30 – 8:00 AM
🥣
Oats Porridge + Walnuts + Banana
Steel-cut or rolled oats cooked in low-fat milk or almond milk. Top with 4 walnuts and half a banana. Oats help regulate blood sugar — key for PCOS. Selenium in walnuts directly supports thyroid hormone production.
✔ Low GI ✔ Selenium boost ✔ Hormone friendly
🌿 Mid-Morning · 10:30 AM
🍎
Apple + 1 tsp Peanut Butter
Keeps blood sugar stable between meals. Peanut butter adds healthy fat and protein — both help reduce PCOS-related insulin resistance. Avoid biscuits or chips at this time.
✔ Blood sugar stable
🌞 Lunch · 1:00 PM
🍛
Brown Rice + Egg Curry + Carrot Salad
Brown rice is better than white for PCOS — lower GI, more fibre. Egg curry (2 eggs) gives iodine and protein, both essential to make thyroid hormones. Raw carrot salad with lemon and cumin on the side for fibre and vitamins.
✔ Iodine rich ✔ High protein ✘ Avoid white rice
🌤 Evening · 4:30 PM
🥤
Coconut Water + Roasted Chana (handful)
Coconut water rehydrates and balances electrolytes. Roasted chana gives plant protein and zinc — both helpful for PCOS hormonal balance. Skip sugary drinks at this time completely.
✔ Zinc + Electrolytes
🌙 Dinner · 7:30 PM
🍲
Grilled Chicken + Vegetable Soup + 1 Chapati
Lean grilled chicken (not fried) provides selenium and iodine. Light vegetable soup with spinach, carrots and tomatoes. One whole wheat chapati only. Eat dinner before 8 PM — late eating disrupts thyroid function overnight.
✔ Lean protein ✘ No fried food

🚫 Avoid Today

Refined sugar Maida / White flour Raw cabbage / Broccoli Cold drinks / Soda Late night eating

🌟 Monday Wellness Tips

  • Take thyroid medicine on empty stomach — wait 30–45 min before eating breakfast
  • Warm lemon water first thing in the morning kick-starts digestion and metabolism
  • A 20-min walk after lunch improves insulin sensitivity — great daily habit for PCOS
  • Sleep by 10:30 PM — thyroid hormones and PCOS recovery happen during deep sleep
2Tue
Tuesday — Gut Heal Day 🌱Full Vegetarian · PCOS hormone reset
🟢 VEG DAY
☀️ Breakfast · 7:30 – 8:00 AM
🫘
Sprouted Moong Dal Dosa + Coconut Chutney
Sprouted moong is a powerhouse — high protein, easy to digest, very low GI. Perfect for PCOS blood sugar management. Coconut chutney adds healthy fat. Avoid regular white rice dosa today — too high GI.
✔ High plant protein ✔ Gut friendly
🌿 Mid-Morning · 10:30 AM
🥛
Low-fat Curd (1 cup) + Flaxseeds (1 tsp)
Curd is a probiotic that improves gut health — very important for thyroid patients (70% of immune system lives in the gut). Flaxseeds contain lignans that help balance PCOS oestrogen levels naturally. Make this a daily habit!
✔ Probiotic ✔ Hormone balance
🌞 Lunch · 1:00 PM
🍱
Millet (Ragi/Bajra) Chapati + Dal + Beetroot Salad
Millet rotis are excellent for PCOS — mineral rich, low GI and gut-friendly. Dal (lentil curry) gives plant protein and iron. Beetroot salad with lemon — beets improve thyroid blood circulation and boost iron absorption significantly.
✔ Iron source ✔ Low GI millet ✘ No white rice today
🌤 Evening · 4:30 PM
🧃
Amla Juice (1 glass) + Pumpkin + Sunflower Seeds
Amla (Indian gooseberry) is one of the best foods for thyroid — powerful antioxidant and rich in Vitamin C which boosts iron absorption. Pumpkin seeds provide zinc which is critical for PCOS hormonal repair. Have a small handful.
✔ Antioxidant ✔ Zinc for PCOS
🌙 Dinner · 7:30 PM
🥘
Palak Paneer + 1 Millet Roti + Cucumber Raita
Spinach gives iron and folate — both important when you feel weak and tired. Paneer is a complete protein and helps keep you full all night. Cucumber raita soothes the gut. Keep oil minimal when cooking. Light and nourishing!
✔ Iron + Folate ✔ Complete protein

🚫 Avoid Tuesday

Excess soy products Raw cruciferous veg Processed cheese Sugar sweets / Mithai Packaged snacks

🌟 Tuesday Wellness Tips

  • Flaxseeds are a top food for PCOS — add 1 tsp daily to curd, smoothies or roti dough
  • Avoid eating raw cabbage, cauliflower and broccoli — they suppress thyroid when raw (cooked is perfectly fine)
  • Do 10 minutes of light stretching or yoga — it lowers cortisol which directly worsens PCOS symptoms
  • Drink water 30 min before meals, not during — it improves digestion significantly
3Wed
Wednesday — Strength Day 💪Non-veg allowed · Iron & Energy focus
☀️ Breakfast · 7:30 – 8:00 AM
🍳
2 Boiled Eggs + Whole Wheat Toast + Orange
Eggs are one of the best thyroid foods — they contain iodine (in yolk) and selenium, both needed to make thyroid hormones. Orange gives Vitamin C which helps your body absorb iron better. A perfect combo when you feel weak and drained!
✔ Iodine + Selenium ✔ Iron absorption
🌿 Mid-Morning · 10:30 AM
🫐
Mixed Berry Smoothie (banana + berries + almond milk)
Berries are anti-inflammatory and antioxidant-rich — they reduce chronic inflammation that worsens both PCOS and thyroid conditions. Banana gives potassium and natural energy. No sugar needed — the fruit is sweet enough!
✔ Anti-inflammatory
🌞 Lunch · 1:00 PM
🐟
Grilled Fish (Tuna / Rohu / Salmon) + Brown Rice + Steamed Veggies
Fish is the single best food for thyroid — loaded with iodine, omega-3 fatty acids and selenium. Omega-3 also reduces PCOS inflammation and helps regulate hormones and periods. Have steamed beans, carrot and zucchini on the side.
✔ Omega-3 ✔ Best thyroid food ✘ No deep fry
🌤 Evening · 4:30 PM
🥜
Almonds (8–10 nos) + Green Tea (no sugar)
Almonds are high in magnesium — magnesium deficiency is very common in PCOS and causes fatigue and cramps. Green tea contains EGCG which may help reduce androgen (male hormone) levels that cause PCOS symptoms. No sugar in tea!
✔ Magnesium for PCOS ✔ Anti-androgen
🌙 Dinner · 7:30 PM
🍜
Chicken Clear Soup + 2 Millet Rotis + Tomato Salad
Clear chicken soup is warming, easy to digest and protein-rich without being heavy at night. Millet rotis keep the GI low. Tomato salad with olive oil and herbs. Keep dinner light and warm. No heavy gravies at night.
✔ Light protein dinner

🚫 Avoid Wednesday

Fried fish / Chicken fry White rice in excess Coffee after 2 PM Sugary fruit juices

🌟 Wednesday Wellness Tips

  • If you feel very weak and tired, it may be low iron — ask your doctor to check ferritin and TSH together
  • Avoid coffee within 4 hours of taking thyroid medicine — it blocks medicine absorption
  • Fish 2–3 times a week is far better than thyroid or omega-3 supplements for natural recovery
  • A 15-min walk after dinner improves insulin sensitivity overnight and helps PCOS weight management
4Thu
Thursday — Balance & Glow ✨Non-veg allowed · Skin + Hormone focus
☀️ Breakfast · 7:30 – 8:00 AM
🌾
Ragi (Finger Millet) Idli + Sambar with Drumstick
Ragi is excellent for thyroid — high in calcium, iron and amino acids. Ragi idlis are lighter than regular idli and give steady energy. Sambar with drumstick (moringa) adds powerful thyroid support. Moringa is one of the best foods for thyroid health!
✔ Calcium + Iron ✔ Moringa thyroid boost
🌿 Mid-Morning · 10:30 AM
🥝
Kiwi + Papaya (small bowl)
Kiwi is one of the highest Vitamin C fruits — boosts iron absorption and fights inflammation. Papaya contains papain enzyme which aids digestion and helps with PCOS-related hormonal skin issues like acne and oiliness. Great skin food!
✔ Skin glow ✔ Digestive aid
🌞 Lunch · 1:00 PM
🥚
Egg Bhurji (2 eggs, turmeric) + Brown Rice + Spinach Soup
Egg bhurji with onion, tomato and turmeric (minimal oil) is perfect for PCOS. Turmeric is deeply anti-inflammatory. Spinach soup adds iron and folate which fight the tiredness and weakness you feel. A very healing lunch!
✔ Iron + Folate ✔ Turmeric anti-inflame
🌤 Evening · 4:30 PM
🫚
Hummus + Cucumber / Carrot Sticks
Hummus made from chickpeas is excellent plant protein and low GI. The sesame (tahini) in hummus provides calcium and healthy fat. Crunchy vegetables add fibre which is essential for PCOS hormonal detox through digestion. Very filling snack!
✔ Fibre + Protein combo
🌙 Dinner · 7:30 PM
🍲
Chicken Bone Broth Soup + 1 Roti + Fresh Salad
Bone broth is incredibly nourishing when feeling weak — full of collagen, minerals and amino acids supporting thyroid, gut and joint health. Add ginger and turmeric while cooking. Keep the roti to just one. Light and deeply healing dinner.
✔ Gut healing collagen ✔ Mineral rich

🚫 Avoid Thursday

Excess salt (water retention) Processed / Packaged foods Alcohol Excess dairy if acne prone

🌟 Thursday Wellness Tips

  • Moringa (drumstick leaves or powder) is one of the most powerful thyroid support foods — add to sambar or dal every week
  • Turmeric + black pepper in cooking enhances anti-inflammatory absorption by a huge amount — always pair them!
  • PCOS can cause skin issues — drink all 8 glasses of water and consider reducing dairy if you notice breakouts
  • Magnesium-rich foods (almonds, seeds, millet) help with PCOS mood swings, cramps and better sleep quality
5Fri
Friday — Cleanse & Restore 🌸Full Vegetarian · Detox & Weekly Reset
🟢 VEG DAY
☀️ Breakfast · 7:30 – 8:00 AM
🥗
Moong Dal Cheela (2 nos) + Mint Chutney + Guava
Moong dal cheela is a high protein savoury pancake — filling, light and very low GI. Perfect for PCOS mornings when you need slow energy release throughout the day. Mint chutney aids digestion. Guava is high in Vitamin C and helps with PCOS skin.
✔ High protein veg ✔ Slow energy release
🌿 Mid-Morning · 10:30 AM
🌿
Methi (Fenugreek) Water + 6 Soaked Almonds
Soak 1 tsp fenugreek seeds in water overnight — drink the water and eat the seeds in the morning. Methi is a superstar for both PCOS (reduces insulin resistance) and thyroid (reduces inflammation). Soaked almonds are more bioavailable than raw ones.
✔ PCOS insulin support ✔ Thyroid friendly
🌞 Lunch · 1:00 PM
🍛
Rajma / Chickpea Curry + Brown Rice + Onion Salad
Rajma (kidney beans) or chickpeas are among the best PCOS foods — high fibre, plant protein and minerals that support hormonal balance. Brown rice and legume combination gives complete amino acids without any meat. Add cumin and turmeric to the curry.
✔ Complete plant protein ✔ Hormone support
🌤 Evening · 4:30 PM
🍵
Turmeric Milk (Haldi Doodh) + 2 Dates
Warm turmeric milk with a pinch of black pepper and cinnamon is deeply anti-inflammatory and reduces PCOS and thyroid inflammation. Dates give iron and natural sugar to beat the 4 PM energy crash without causing a blood sugar spike. Very comforting!
✔ Anti-inflammatory ✔ Iron from dates
🌙 Dinner · 7:30 PM
🥣
Vegetable Khichdi + Raita + Steamed Beans
Khichdi (rice + moong dal + vegetables) is the ultimate healing meal for thyroid and PCOS patients — easy to digest, balanced macros and deeply comforting. Add turmeric, 1 tsp ghee and cumin. Ghee in small amounts supports thyroid hormone synthesis. Lightest dinner of the week!
✔ Most gut-friendly ✔ Ghee for hormones

🚫 Avoid Friday

Excess soy milk / soy products Carbonated drinks Junk / Fast food Skipping any meal Late night snacks

🌟 Friday Wellness Tips

  • Fenugreek (methi) seeds soaked overnight is one of the most evidence-backed natural PCOS remedies — make it a daily habit!
  • End of week check: Did you drink 8 glasses every day? Consistent hydration matters more than being perfect on some days
  • Cinnamon in food and warm drinks helps reduce insulin resistance — add it to oats, milk or smoothies daily
  • Sleep is medicine for both thyroid and PCOS — poor sleep raises cortisol and worsens both. Aim for 7–8 hours every night 💤
